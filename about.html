Meet the DuckSquad

We’re a team of senior DevOps engineers passionate about calm, scalable, resilient infrastructure. DuckLabs is where cloud meets composure.

Equal parts architect and adventurer, Spencer has a knack for simplifying complex problems and automating chaos out of existence. A proud father of four ducklings, Spencer balances DevOps wizardry with family life and a deep appreciation for fine whiskey (single malt only, thank you very much). When he’s not optimizing cloud deployments or perfecting CI/CD flows, you’ll find him gliding across calm waters with a neat glass of whiskey in one wing and a plan in the other. "Quack less. Build more.™"
